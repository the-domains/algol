Alberto Krone-Martins - Personal Webpage

Trying to understand the Universe through Space Astrometry – and helping to make the ESA-Gaia satellite data processing and analysis consortium (DPAC) a success.

An astronomer, working at the SIM group of the Faculdade de Ciências da Universidade de Lisboa. Most of my research efforts are dedicated to Space Astrometry.

Outside the academic world, my main hobbies are amateur astronomy and outreach, computing and music.

I am also helping to shape the next step in Space Astrometry: aiming to unravel Dark Matter clumping in small scales, Habitable Exoplanets and the very own nature of Space-Time.
